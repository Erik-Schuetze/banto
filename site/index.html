How to use Bantō

Adding Columns

Click the "add column" button on the right side of the board. Type your column name and press Enter to create it.

Adding Items

Click the + button at the bottom of any column to add a new item. Each item has a title (bold) and a notes section.

Editing

Click on any item title or notes to edit them. Press Enter to save, or Shift + Enter to add a new line within the text.

Deleting Columns

Hover over a column title to reveal the × button. Click it to delete the column. Warning: This will delete all items in that column!

Data Persistence

Your board is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. Your data stays on your device and persists between sessions.
